The Upstream Approach

Wellness support, moved ahead of the crisis.

An anonymous wellness and peer-support app, built specifically for first responders — and the dispatchers, families, and teammates who carry it with them.

No accounts. No tracking. Just real support, a tap away, before a hard stretch becomes a crisis.

🔒

Anonymous by design

No login, no name, no tracking. The system never knows who is using it — because trust is what makes people reach out.

🤝

Built-in peer connection

Quietly check on a teammate, reach the person you trust, or activate support after a bad call — without making it a big thing.

🧰

Tools for the hard moment

Grounding, breathing, journaling, and decompression tools that work right on the device — whenever you need them.

👪

For the whole team

Responders, dispatchers, civilian staff, and the families who carry the load too — support that includes everyone.

Crisis access is always on

Hardwired 988, 211, and peer lines — one tap from anywhere in the app, working whether or not anything else does. Even with limited signal, you can still call 911.
